Libaray facilities

Libraries are an integral part of our school setup which help in the spread of education. GVM School Charnal Sehore has excellent and well equipped libraries. Numerous books, arranged in open bookshelves is a treat to the eye and are very inviting. The school houses separate libraries for Junior college and Primary/Middle wing which is well-equipped with technology and software to provide a robust backbone to the e-library.

Students and staff can browse through the extensive book stock (i.e fiction, non fiction and reference study material), CDs, daily newspaper (English & Hindi), latest magazines and journals on different subjects. The libraries stand as unique ‘Knowledge Centres’ and ‘Hubs of learning’ that offer access to essential and specialized information resources and services to meet the growing demands of the present day curriculum
